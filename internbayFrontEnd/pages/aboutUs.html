About InternBay

Empowering the next generation of professionals by connecting students with meaningful internship opportunities worldwide.

Our Vision

At InternBay, we believe in simplifying the hiring journey and creating a platform where careers take shape. Our mission is to provide a global job platform, making a positive impact on society by connecting professionals with opportunities that align with their aspirations.

We envision a world where talent knows no boundaries, where students can seamlessly transition from academia to industry, and where companies can discover the brightest minds regardless of geographical constraints.

Our Journey

2020
The Beginning

InternBay was founded with a vision to bridge the gap between students and industry, starting with 50 partner companies.

Rapid Growth

Expanded operations to 25 countries and introduced AI-powered matching algorithms for better job-student compatibility.

Innovation & Features

Launched resume checker, video interviews, and career guidance features. Achieved 50,000+ successful placements.
